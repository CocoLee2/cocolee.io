I am Coco K. Lee, a B.S. Computer Science graduate from the Paul G. Allen School at the University of Washington.

I am currently a research assistant at the Computing Intelligence & Education Lab (CIEL) , advised by Prof. Yang Shi at Utah State University, exploring how AI and LLMs can improve learning efficiency and feedback in computing education.

I previously worked with Prof. R. Benjamin Shapiro in the LCI Lab, contributing to projects in multimodal UI design tools, machine learning education, and dog-wearable sensing technology.

I am also a part-time CS instructor at Coding Mind Academy, teaching K–12 students programming and computational thinking.
