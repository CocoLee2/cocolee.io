About Me 👋

Hi! I’m Coco K. Lee. I earned a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Washington (Paul G. Allen School).

I’m broadly interested in Generative AI, Computing Education, Human–Computer Interaction, and Linguistics.

I previously worked with Prof. R. Benjamin Shapiro in the LCI (Learning, Computing, and Imagination) Lab.

I love teaching. I’m currently a part-time CS instructor at Coding Mind Academy, and I’ve taught math/coding in after-school programs.
